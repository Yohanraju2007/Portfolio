Who is Yohan?

I'm Yohan Raju, a passionate digital creator and marketer from Kochi, Kerala. Currently pursuing my first year at Kristu Jayanti University, Bangalore, I blend academic knowledge with real-world creative execution.

I work as a Digital Marketing Executive for a café, managing social media presence, creating content, and designing eye-catching visuals that drive engagement. I also capture moments behind the lens — from portraits to professional photoshoots.

My toolkit spans poster design, video editing, content writing, and social media strategy — all delivered with a premium, intentional aesthetic.
